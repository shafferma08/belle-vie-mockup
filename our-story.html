Our Story

Where faith, nature, and holistic care converge to nurture your spirit.

The Founder's Story

"Come as you are. Leave renewed."

Hello, my name is Shawna Fearneley, and I’m the founder of Belle Vie Hair Spa in Westchase, Tampa. I created this space as more than just a salon—it’s a sanctuary. A place where beauty, wellness, and faith intertwine.

At Belle Vie, we believe in caring for the whole person: mind, body, and spirit. Our approach is luxurious yet clean, combining the power of organic ingredients with the comfort of intentional, faith-filled service. Every product we use is carefully selected to be as natural and non-toxic as possible—because your health and the health of our planet matter deeply.

Most importantly, we are proud to be believers in Jesus Christ, and we carry that faith into the heart of everything we do. From how we care for our clients to the peaceful energy in our space, Belle Vie is a reflection of God’s grace and the beauty He placed within each of us.

Whether you're visiting for a transformative hair treatment, a wellness service, or simply a moment to breathe—we welcome you with open arms.
